Frequently Asked Questions

what is a Give-N-Take Hub site?? +

This website serves as a platform where individuals can exchange goods and personal belongings for other items they require. The unique aspect is that the exchange occurs without involving money; instead, it follows a direct barter system, where goods are swapped for goods. Additionally, through our website, you have the option to choose an item as a gift or borrow it.

What's new in a Give-N-Take Hub website if we can exchange goodson social networking sites like Facebook? +

The novelty lies in the website's mechanism, where the website itself searches for the desired goods on your behalf, and connects them to the items you wish to exchange. Here's an example to illustrate how the website operates: Let's assume you own a Samsung phone and would like to try an iPhone. When you register your request on our platform, our system will search through all the registered items from our visitors who own an iPhone and wish to exchange it for a Samsung phone. Then, your item will be linked to all the available iPhones on our platform, and you will receive notifications accordingly. In case no iPhone is currently available at the time of your request, our system will link your request to the first iPhone that gets added and send you a notification. Additionally, you have the option to take the desired item as a gift or even borrow it.

1. Save valuable time by quickly navigating through comments in communication groups until you locate the specific information you seek.
2. You have the flexibility to select more than one desired item for exchange. For instance, you can register a request to trade your Samsung device for a Huawei, iPhone, or even a laptop.
3. Unlike in communication groups where posts become more difficult accessible over time, the Give-N-Take Hub site operates differently. It employs a mechanism that continuously sorts and connects items, allowing you to add your request today and still find what you're looking for on the same day, tomorrow, or even after a month. This eliminates any time constraints; you only need to follow alerts, which typically take less than a minute.
4. Gift or Borrowing Options: You can discover and obtain the desired goods as a gift or, as a borrowed item.

Is a login required to use the Give-N-Take Hub website? +

Logging in is necessary to link the additional items to your personal account and enable owner communication. This makes it possible for parties participating in the exchange process to ask specific questions and exchange information. It guarantees a smooth exchange experience and enables consumers to get the information they need before to completing the transaction.

How will the goods be delivered to the two parties to the barter? +

Through the site's chat feature, the two parties engaged in the barter can utilize it to coordinate and arrange the meeting time and location for exchanging the goods. This chat functionality enables effective communication and ensures that both parties can conveniently manage the logistics of the exchange.
