ABOUT US

As junior Software Engineering students who determined to make dramatic change in the world, we have developed unique idea for our web development course at UPM and for the marketing industry as a general, which is website serves as an online marketplace that facilitates and provides a set of three primary services, namely, borrowing, bartering, and gifting. Whether you're looking to one of these services, our platform makes it easy and accessible to you!.

Our Idea:

According to the well-known story of a man who gets a house as a result of exchanging much stuff, starting from a red paper clip, then a door handle and a food grill, until he exchanged a role in a movie for a house. From this story, our website was born to open the business door for everyone. Our users can exchange something they no longer want but that has value to others, provide a service in exchange for the product they want. With our website, there is no place for excuses to start your business and fulfill your dreams!

Our Goal:

Our main goal with this website is to encourage people to initiative and inculcate this in them, but at the same time, the website helps spread the principles of reuse. Instead of people throwing their property, they can display the things that are in a good condition on the website and people come to barter or borrow them, and this idea helps to create a spirit of cooperation and initiative, but at the same time helps to preserve the environment, this is in line with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030.
